WebSoroban is a browser IDE for Soroban smart contracts on the Stellar testnet.
You write Rust, compile to Wasm, deploy, and call your contract without
installing the Rust toolchain, the Stellar CLI, or managing a keypair.

Sign in and you get a testnet wallet that is created and funded for you. From
there, the loop is: edit code, **Compile**, **Deploy**, then **Invoke** your
contract's functions with typed inputs.
